# TweetOS — Grok Packet Playbook & Templates v3.0
## Refined, Self-Learning, Mobile-Optimized

> This document details the exact copy-paste inputs (Given to Grok) and the structured output formats (Taken from Grok) used to interface with Grok's real-time X search.

---

## HOW SELF-LEARNING WORKS

The packet generator pulls from four data sources that grow over time:

1. **Performance Library** — posted tweets + notes from the library.
2. **Flop Log** — archived or low-score drafts to avoid repeating mistakes.
3. **Engagement Memory** — who replied, what formats worked.
4. **Learning Notes** — the running manual takeaway log injected into every session.
